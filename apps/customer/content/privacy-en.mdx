## Admin Audit Log IP Collection

### Purpose

We collect IP addresses of administrators (Admin) when actions are performed through the back-office management system for:
- Security monitoring of the system
- Anti-fraud measures for administrative operations
- Security incident response and investigation

### Legal Basis

This data collection is based on **Legitimate Interest**:
- **Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)** Section 24(5) — Legitimate Interest basis
- **GDPR** Article 6(1)(f) — Legitimate Interests

### Retention Period

| Period | IP Address Handling |
|--------|-------------------|
| 0–30 days | Full (raw) IP address retained for incident response |
| 31–90 days | Partially masked — IPv4 to /24, IPv6 to /48 |
| Over 90 days | IP address deleted (set to NULL) — audit log record retained |

### Data Subject Rights

You have the following rights:
- **Right of Access** — Request to see the IP address data we have collected about you
- **Right to Object** — Object to the collection of your IP address
- **Right to Erasure** — Request deletion of your IP address before the 90-day retention cap, per GDPR Article 17
